## 6. Firebase Specific Declarations (Add this to Data Safety & Privacy Policy)

Since you are using **Firebase Auth, Firestore, Database, Storage, and App Check**, you must declare the following:

### A. Data Safety Form (Detailed Breakdown)

When asked **"Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?"**, select **Yes**.

**1. Personal Information**
*   **Email Address** (Collected, Ephemeral processing, App functionality, Account management)
    *   *Source:* Firebase Authentication.
*   **User IDs** (Collected, App functionality, Account management)
    *   *Source:* Firebase Authentication (UIDs).

**2. Photo and Videos**
*   **Photos** (Collected, App functionality)
    *   *Source:* Firebase Storage (Profile images).

**3. App Info and Performance**
*   **Device or other IDs** (Collected, App functionality, Fraud prevention, security, and compliance)
    *   *Source:* Firebase App Check & Auth. (Used to verify device integrity).

### B. Privacy Policy Additions

You **MUST** add these clauses to your Privacy Policy URL page:

> **Service Providers**
> We use the following third-party services to facilitate our Service:
>
> *   **Google Firebase:** We use Firebase services provided by Google.
>     *   **Firebase Authentication:** To manage user accounts securely.
>     *   **Cloud Firestore & Realtime Database:** To store and sync user data (comments, book details) in real-time.
>     *   **Cloud Storage for Firebase:** To store user-uploaded content such as profile pictures.
>     *   **Firebase App Check:** To protect our API resources from abuse by preventing unauthorized clients from accessing our backend resources.
>
> For more information on how Google Firebase handles user data, please visit the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### C. App Check Specifics
*   If you encounter issues with "Unverified App" or API limits, ensure you have registered your **SHA-256 fingerprint** in the Firebase Console Project Settings.
*   For the Play Console, typically App Check doesn't require extra tick-boxes outside of "Device or other IDs" in Data Safety for security purposes.
